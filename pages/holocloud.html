Concept

Holo-cloud is a data management and security product that allows users to visualize the entirety of their personal assets and control what they would like to protect most. Holo-cloud aims to create an easy and convenient data security solution that helps users manage their data, but also informs and educates users to value their sensitive information more.

With the evolution of technology, real people are exposed to being more vulnerable.

On average victims of abuse will attempt leaving their abuser seven times before being successful.

Problem

Users lack an understanding as well as undervalue their data making themselves more vulnerable to cyber security attacks.

These factors causes stagnation in the management of information and development of protocol.

Solution

Holo-cloud is a data management and security product that allows users to visualize the entirety of their personal assets and control what they would like to protect most.

There are two aspects of Holo-cloud: the application for the uploading, accessing, and managing account information and documents you have stored within the Vault and Holo, a hologram to help users visualize their data scripts across external and cloud-based platforms.

Project Process

We are currently in the development phase of our project.

We are currently in the second half of our senior project. At this phase in our project we are working on developing a working hologram using leap motion. We will then test our hologram with users and evolve our product for the final delivery.
