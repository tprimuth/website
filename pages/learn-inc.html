Web design

3D Learning experience

Design a mock webpage featuring the four elements, earth, air, fire and water.

For this assignment I created a 3-dimensional interactive science platform for kids. I wanted to create something that would mimic the in-person tactile experience of learning given the global climate of remote learning due to the pandemic. Using cinema4d I created a 3-dimensional scene for the homepage that features hover interactions and On-click function to explore each element further in their own scene/page.

Moving forward in the development stage I am working with three.js to bring this 3-dimensional experience to life!
